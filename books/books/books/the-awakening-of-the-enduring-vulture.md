# The Awakening of the Enduring Vulture

## Author
Elizabeth Awodola

## Genre
Children's Adventure Fiction | African Fantasy | Mystery

## Description
When young Ifeoluwa uncovers a mysterious wooden mask beneath an ancient baobab tree, she is drawn into a world of whispers, visions, and secrets buried by time.

The mask holds the spirit of Aderogba, a powerful seer betrayed by the very people who feared his gift. To uncover the truth, Ifeoluwa and her friends must face dangerous trials, unravel hidden mysteries, and make a choice that could awaken—or silence forever—the Enduring Vulture.

A thrilling adventure of courage, friendship, history, and destiny, perfect for young readers who enjoy mystery, African folklore, and epic tales of good versus evil.
