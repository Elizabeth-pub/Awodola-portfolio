## 🔍 Research Focus

My research interests lie at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Financial Fraud Detection, Risk Analytics, Financial Crime Prevention, and Data-Driven Decision Making in Emerging Markets.

I am particularly interested in developing machine learning and data analytics solutions that can strengthen fraud detection systems, improve financial security, and enhance regulatory effectiveness within banking and financial institutions, especially in Africa and other emerging economies.

I am a research-driven professional with a background in Accounting, Banking Operations, Fraud Investigation, Financial Analysis, and Educational Publishing.

I am also a published author writing under the name Elizabeth Awodola, with multiple books available on Amazon.

My long-term goal is to contribute to the advancement of AI-powered solutions for fraud detection and financial risk management while pursuing doctoral research in Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Financial Technology (FinTech), and Risk Analytics.

## 📊 Research Projects

### Fraud Detection Using Machine Learning

This project explores how machine learning techniques can be applied to identify fraudulent financial transactions and support early detection of suspicious activities within banking systems.

**Key Areas:**

* Data preprocessing
* Anomaly detection
* Classification models
* Fraud risk scoring

---

### BVN and Fraud Risk Analysis

This project evaluates the effectiveness of the Bank Verification Number (BVN) system in reducing fraud within the Nigerian financial ecosystem.

**Key Areas:**

* Identity verification
* Fraud reduction strategies
* Financial system security
* Customer risk profiling

---

### AI Framework for Financial Crime Detection

A conceptual framework designed to integrate Artificial Intelligence, predictive analytics, and risk modelling into fraud detection systems for emerging markets.

**Key Areas:**

* Machine learning
* Predictive analytics
* Financial crime detection
* Risk assessment systems
