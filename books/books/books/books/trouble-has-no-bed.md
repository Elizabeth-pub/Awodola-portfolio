# Trouble Has No Bed

## Author
Elizabeth Awodola

## Genre
Children's Fiction | Moral Story | Educational Literature

## Description
Meet Tambo, a playful little monkey whose love for fun often leads him into unexpected trouble. From breaking bird nests to creating chaos in the marketplace, his adventures are filled with laughter, lessons, and consequences.

As Tambo begins to understand how his actions affect others, he learns important lessons about kindness, responsibility, and making better choices.

A heartwarming story designed for young readers, teaching character development, empathy, and personal growth through engaging storytelling and memorable adventures.
